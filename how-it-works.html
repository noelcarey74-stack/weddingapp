How It Works

WeddingApp.ie is designed to be simple from start to finish. From your first enquiry to the final setup and post-wedding access, everything is kept clear, elegant and easy to manage.

The step-by-step process

The goal is to take the stress out of the digital side of the wedding and give couples something that feels polished, modern and supported.

1

Get in touch

The first step is simple. You contact Noel Carey with your wedding date, what type of setup you would like, and any features you want included.

2

Plan your setup

We go through the main details of your wedding app, including photo uploads, table plan access, guest book features, QR code entry and any extra touches you want.

3

Your wedding app is prepared

Your page is built in a clean and elegant format, with the information and features your guests need in one place.

4

Share it with guests

Guests can access the wedding app by direct link or QR code, making it quick and easy for them to use on the day.

5

Support with changes

If something changes before the wedding, including guest details or table plan updates, you are not left to sort it all out alone. Help is there to keep things accurate.

6

Stay live after the wedding

The wedding app remains live for 1 month after the big day, giving guests time to upload photos and leave their messages.

What makes the process different

WeddingApp.ie is not just a digital product. It is a service with real support behind it, designed to make life easier for couples rather than giving them more work to do.

Direct support
You deal directly with Noel Carey rather than being left with a self-service system.
Easy for guests
Guests can quickly access the wedding app, find what they need and enjoy the day.
Clean and organised
Photos, messages and practical information are kept together in one place.

After the wedding

The experience does not have to end on the wedding day itself. There is still time afterwards for guests to add photos and messages.

1 month of live access
Your wedding app stays available for 1 month after the wedding so guests can continue uploading and sharing.
Printed keepsake option
Selected photos and guest book messages can be prepared as a printed keepsake after the wedding.
Delivered within 2 weeks
Printed keepsake options can be arranged for delivery within 2 weeks after the wedding.

A simple process with a personal touch

WeddingApp.ie is built to make the digital side of your wedding feel easy, organised and fully supported from beginning to end.
