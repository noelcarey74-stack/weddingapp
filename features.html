Features

WeddingApp.ie gives couples a clean, elegant digital space for the most useful parts of the wedding day. Everything is designed to be easy for guests to use and easy for couples to manage, with personal support behind the service.

The main features couples can use

These are the core parts of the service that help keep the day organised, modern and memorable for both the couple and their guests.

📸

Guest Photo Uploads

Give guests a simple place to upload photos from the day so that moments from family and friends are not lost across phones and messages.

📍

Table Plan Access

Help guests find their table quickly and easily without confusion, making arrivals and seating feel much smoother.

📖

Digital Guest Book

Create a special place where guests can leave messages, memories and warm words for the couple before, during or after the wedding.

📱

QR Code Entry

Guests can open the wedding app quickly using a QR code, making access simple and convenient on the day.

🗓️

Wedding Day Details

Share useful information in one place, including timings, guest guidance, or any details you want people to have close at hand.

🤝

Personal Support

You are not left to manage it all alone. Noel Carey works with you directly and helps with setup, updates and changes where needed.

Extra value that makes the service stand out

WeddingApp.ie is not just about having a digital page live for the day. It is designed to be useful before the wedding, during the celebration and after it as well.

Help with changes
If your table plan or guest details need to be adjusted, support is there to help keep everything up to date.
1 month post-wedding access
The wedding app stays live for 1 month after the wedding, giving guests time to upload photos and leave messages.
Clean and manageable service
After the live period ends, content can be removed unless otherwise agreed, helping keep the service tidy and practical.

Printed keepsake option

For couples who want something physical to hold onto after the wedding, WeddingApp.ie can also offer a more personal finishing touch.

Printed guest book messages
Guest messages can be prepared as a printed keepsake after the wedding.
Selected printed photographs
A chosen set of favourite photographs can also be printed rather than trying to include every single upload.
Delivered after the wedding
Printed keepsake options can be arranged for delivery within 2 weeks after the wedding.

Everything in one elegant place

WeddingApp.ie combines useful digital tools with personal support, helping couples create a guest experience that feels modern, organised and memorable.
